About MiniApps

Created by a software developer originally from Iowa, USA, MiniApps aims to provide simple solutions to problems we encounter every day.

The apps will always be open source, privacy oriented, and with minimalism at the forefront.

As this is currently a one-person team, things will slowly become developed.

Languages you can expect to see are C, C++, C#, Python, Java, JavaScript, and web development.

Any questions, suggestions, or inquiries can be directly sent to the MiniApps Twitter account @MiniAppsBrand.
